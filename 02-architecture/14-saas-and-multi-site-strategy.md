# SaaS and Multi-site Strategy

Do not force SaaS in v1. Design for later.

## Initial mode

Single installation, multi-site-ready data boundaries where low-cost.

## Later modes

- self-hosted single site;
- self-hosted multi-site;
- SaaS multi-tenant;
- white-label deployments.

Tenant/site isolation must be explicit before enabling multi-tenant production.
